The X·FACTOR of Xoriant

Next, in support of these innovations, we have codified and standardized the engineering methodologies, implementation best practices, measurement methods, and early warning mechanisms, while leaving room for continuous enhancements. This is the “X·FACTOR” – the secret sauce of Xoriant – that ensures robustness, extensibility, and continuity for all the technology we deliver.
